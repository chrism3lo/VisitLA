Staples Center

Go to the Staples Center (now called Crypto.com Arena) go and watch the Lakers or Clippers play.
The tickets can cost anywhere from $80-$1000. The age is between 6-50 yrs old.

Dodgers Stadium

Go watch a Dodgers game and eat a famous Dodger dog. LA is home to the MLB team
the LA Dodgers. The age is between 6-50 yrs old.

Hollywood Walk of Fame

Go to Hollywood Boulevard and look at the Walk of Fame and see stars of celebrities like
Michael Jackson, Elvis Presley, Vicente Fernandez and so many others. The cost is free as
it is public property. Ages range between 10-70 years old.

Santa Monica Pier

Visit Santa Monica Pier and explore the beaches, outdoor shopping center, as well as the beach.
The Pier has many different options for both entertainment and food. If you drive you will have to pay
for parking which can run from anywhere to $10-$40 depending on how long you stay. As well as if you want get on the amusement rides that will cost aswell. The Age range is all ages.
